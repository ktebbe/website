Digital Puzzle Hunts

A free, online, multi-step puzzle hunt!

I wanted to design a fun experience that anyone with a computer and Internet could do at home. All you need to do is investigate/download the two PDFs (the first clue and the password-protected final puzzle), be curious, and solve it!

P.S. In some browsers, you can use the arrows in the top-right corner of the files to download the PDFs. Otherwise, the hyperlinks in the hunt description will open each PDF in a new tab.

Your 18th Birthday

On your 18th birthday, you get a letter from your Grandma Maya, who died before you were born. Can you decipher her letter to unlock the end prize? There are four puzzles to solvex this hunt. Good luck!
