Project Context and Description

As part of my internship with United Way of Metropolitan Chicago, I redesigned a schedule for an upcoming seminar with all AmeriCorps members. I wanted to visually convey the day's progression and each event’s duration. Even a quick glance to the vertical line in the middle of the page gives readers a sense of what’s to come for the rest of the day. The border added a sense of formality, which was appropriate given the size of the audience and the number of guest speakers.
